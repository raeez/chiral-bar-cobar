# Analytic Realization Criterion (conj:analytic-realization) -- Status Assessment

## Statement

Let V be a unitary full VOA satisfying:
(i)   its quasi-primary correlators satisfy conformal OS axioms (Adamo-Moriwaki-Tanimoto),
(ii)  its graded pieces have polynomial spectral growth,
(iii) its intertwining OPE maps satisfy HS-sewing after exponential weight damping.

Then V admits:
- a sewing envelope V_alg -> V^sew in IndHilb,
- a conformally flat 2-disk algebra F_V,
- a genus-zero analytic Koszul duality package, and
- a higher-genus coderived shadow Q_bullet^an(V).

## What Is Proved vs What Remains Conjectural

### PROVED:

1. **HS-sewing criterion for the entire standard landscape**
   (thm:general-hs-sewing, cor:hs-sewing-standard-landscape):
   Polynomial OPE growth + subexponential sector growth implies convergence
   at all genera. This proves hypothesis (iii) for all universal algebras
   in the standard landscape (Heisenberg, affine KM, Virasoro, W_N, betagamma,
   lattice VOAs).

2. **Heisenberg sewing theorem** (thm:heisenberg-sewing, target A_an):
   The sewing envelope of H_k is Sym(A^2(D)) (Bergman space symmetric algebra).
   The completed bar differential is the Gaussian collision operator.
   Every closed amplitude is a Fredholm determinant on the one-particle
   Bergman space. Clause (i) proved elsewhere (Moriwaki), clauses (ii)-(iv)
   proved here.

3. **Lattice sewing envelope** (thm:lattice-sewing, target B_an):
   Every positive-definite even lattice VOA admits a canonical sewing envelope
   with charge-refined Hilbert sectors. Promoted from conjecture to theorem.

4. **Conformal OS axioms** for unitary full VOAs under energy/spectral growth
   and local C_1-cofiniteness (Adamo-Moriwaki-Tanimoto 2024).

5. **Conformally flat factorization homology** in IndHilb by left Kan
   extension from conformally flat disk algebras (Moriwaki 2026a, 2026b).

### WHAT SPECIFICALLY REMAINS CONJECTURAL:

The conjecture is NOT about whether HS-sewing holds (that is proved). It is
about the CONSTRUCTION of the four output objects from the three input
hypotheses, for GENERAL unitary full VOAs beyond the explicitly constructed
cases (Heisenberg, lattice).

Precisely, the conjectural content decomposes into three layers:

**Layer 1: Existence of the sewing envelope for interacting algebras.**
For Heisenberg and lattice VOAs, the sewing envelope is constructed explicitly
(Fredholm determinants, theta functions). For interacting algebras (affine KM
at level > 1, Virasoro, W_N), HS-sewing proves that sewing amplitudes CONVERGE,
but constructing the Hausdorff-completed sewing envelope V^sew as an object
in IndHilb requires:
- Identifying the correct locally convex topology on V generated by
  sewing-amplitude seminorms.
- Proving that the Hausdorff completion is an ind-Hilbert space (not merely
  a locally convex space).
- Proving that the VOA structure (vertex operator, vacuum, Virasoro action)
  extends continuously to V^sew.

This is functional analysis, not algebra. The HS-sewing condition provides
the crucial estimates, but assembling them into a completed topological
algebra requires additional work.

**Layer 2: Existence of the conformally flat 2-disk algebra.**
Moriwaki's construction starts from a conformally flat disk algebra and
produces factorization homology by left Kan extension. The conjecture
asserts that every unitary full VOA satisfying (i)-(iii) admits such a
disk algebra. For Heisenberg, the disk algebra is Sym(A^2(D)). For
general VOAs, the disk algebra must be constructed from the sewing
envelope, which requires Layer 1.

The key obstruction (O1 in theorem_analytic_realization_obstruction_engine.py)
is METRIC INDEPENDENCE at chain level. Moriwaki's construction is
metric-dependent (it depends on conformal structure + area form). The
algebraic shadow F_g = kappa * lambda_g is metric-independent. The bridge
requires showing that Moriwaki's IndHilb factorization homology, restricted
to the vacuum sector, descends to a locally constant sheaf on M_g.
Sub-obstruction O1a (anomaly cancellation at chain level) is the hard part;
O1b (conformal class vs metric moduli) is controlled by the Polyakov formula;
O1c (regularization scheme independence) is proved by Bismut-Freed.

**Layer 3: Higher-genus coderived shadow.**
At genus g >= 1, the curvature m_0 = kappa * omega_g is nonzero, so ordinary
derived categories fail. The correct receptacle is the coderived category
D^co(B_g^an(A)-comod). Constructing the analytic coderived shadow requires:
- The analytic bar coalgebra B^an(A) (graph-norm closure of the algebraic bar).
- The coderived passage (from ordinary cohomology to coderived cohomology).
- Proving that the algebraic shadow obstruction tower (proved at all finite
  orders) is compatible with the analytic completion.

This layer is logically downstream of Layers 1 and 2.

### Theta^oc REFORMULATION (concordance lines 9715--9736):

The conjecture can be restated as: the inclusion of the formal open-closed
convolution algebra g^oc_A into an analytic completion g-hat^{oc,an}_A sends
the algebraic MC element Theta^oc_A to a completed MC element
Theta^{oc,an}_A whose genus-g amplitudes converge via trace-class sewing.

This reformulation clarifies that the conjecture is about ANALYTIC
CONTINUATION of the formal MC element -- can the formal power series
in hbar (genus parameter) be summed to a convergent object in the
analytic convolution algebra? For Heisenberg and lattice VOAs, the
answer is yes (proved). For general VOAs, the convergence of the
genus expansion is controlled by the shadow radius rho(A); when
rho >= 1 (Virasoro at c < c* ~ 6.125), the series is a formal
asymptotic expansion, and the "analytic realization" may require
Borel resummation or similar non-perturbative techniques.

algebraic engine and the analytic world of Hilbert spaces, partition functions,
