# Platonic Adjunction Status Assessment

## The Theorem

Theorem (thm:platonic-adjunction, higher_genus_modular_koszul.tex line 24541):
The modular factorization envelope U^mod_X is left adjoint to the
primitive-current functor Prim^mod on cyclically admissible Lie
conformal algebras.  At genus 0 this recovers the Nishinaka-Vicedo
envelope.

Status: **ProvedHere**.  The conjecture was upgraded to a theorem.
The concordance (rem:concordance-platonic-adjunction, line 944)
confirms the proved status.

## What the Proof Establishes

The proof (lines 24600-24672) constructs:

1. **Primitive currents (i)**: Prim^mod(F) := ker(reduced coproduct
   of bar coalgebra) inherits Lie conformal algebra structure via
   the Milnor-Moore theorem for factorization coalgebras.

2. **Modular envelope (ii)**: U^mod_X(L) := Fact_X(L) tensor-hat
   G_mod, where genus-0 is Nishinaka and modular extension appends
   the stable-graph coefficient algebra.

3. **Adjunction (iii)**: Unit (L -> Prim^mod(U^mod_X(L))) and counit
   constructed explicitly.  Genus-0 extension is forced by
   functoriality of the bar construction; uniqueness follows from
   the envelope being generated by L under factorization products
   and modular graph sums.

## What Specifically Was Needed for the Modular Extension

The genus-0 version existed (Nishinaka 2025, Vicedo 2025).  The
modular extension required three ingredients:

(a) **The stable-graph coefficient algebra G_mod** (def:stable-graph-
    coefficient-algebra): a purely combinatorial object encoding
    the modular operad of stable curves.  This is the genus tower.

(b) **The bar-modular operad theorem** (thm:bar-modular-operad):
    the bar complex B^(g,n)(A) is an algebra over FCom (Feynman
    transform of the commutative modular operad).  This makes
    the bar construction functorial at all genera, which is
    essential for the counit construction.

(c) **The MC element bar-intrinsic construction** (thm:mc2-bar-
    intrinsic): Theta_A := D_A - d_0 is automatically MC because
    D_A^2 = 0.  This provides existence of the universal MC class
    on the envelope (cor:envelope-universal-mc).

All three are proved in the manuscript.  The adjunction proof is
self-contained modulo these inputs.

## Relationship to E_1 Primacy and Three Bar Complexes

The platonic adjunction is an E_infty statement: it concerns the
symmetric bar B(A) and the cocommutative coalgebra structure.
The E_1 work (three bar complexes, SC three-sector structure)
is **largely independent** of the adjunction, for the following
reasons:

1. **The adjunction lives on Fact_cyc(X)**, the category of cyclic
   factorization algebras.  This is the E_infty world.  The ordered
   bar B^ord(A) and its FAss-coalgebra structure operate in a
   different category (FAss-coalgebras, not FCom-coalgebras).

2. **Prim^mod uses the symmetric reduced coproduct**, not the
   deconcatenation coproduct.  The primitive elements of a
   cocommutative coalgebra (Milnor-Moore) are different from the
   primitives of a coassociative coalgebra (Cartier-Milnor-Moore).

3. **The SC three-sector structure** (open/closed/mixed) enriches
   the platonic datum from six-fold to eight-fold (def:thqg-
   completed-platonic-datum), but does not change the adjunction
   itself.  The derived center Z^der_ch(A) and the annulus trace
   are additional data on TOP of the adjunction, not modifications
   of it.

However, there is one indirect connection:

4. **An E_1 platonic adjunction** would be the natural ordered
   refinement.  The ordered bar B^ord(A) is an FAss-coalgebra,
   and one could ask whether U^{mod,E_1}_X(L) (an ordered modular
   envelope) is left adjoint to an ordered primitive-current
   functor.  This is not developed in the manuscript but would
   be the natural E_1 counterpart.  The E_1 primacy theorem
   (thm:e1-primacy) establishes the surjection av: g^{mod,E_1} ->>
   g^mod, which would intertwine the two adjunctions.

## Assessment

The platonic adjunction is **proved and self-contained**.  The
E_1 primacy and three-bar-complex work do not affect the proof
or its scope.  They suggest a natural E_1 refinement (an ordered
platonic adjunction) that has not been developed.

The remaining frontier for the platonic adjunction is the
concordance item (concordance.tex line 9663): reformulating the
adjunction in the Theta^oc language, asking whether the open-
closed MC element Theta^oc_A is the universal MC element for the
eight-fold completed datum.  This is an enrichment question, not
a gap in the existing adjunction.
