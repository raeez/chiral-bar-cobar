\begin{abstract}
\itshape
This companion document contains the full concordance with primary
literature and the editorial constitution of the monograph
\emph{Modular Koszul Duality}.  It includes literature comparison
tables, the three-pillar foundational architecture, the three
concentric rings, the Koszulness characterization programme,
cross-volume bridges, the modular homotopy theory programme,
conjecture stratification, research directions, and programme
status assessment.
\end{abstract}
