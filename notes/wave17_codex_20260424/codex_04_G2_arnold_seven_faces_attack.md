Retraction candidate: downgrade strict chain-level equality to residue-presentation equivalence unless a named cochain map from the Arnold connection 1-form to the bar bracket is supplied.

Retraction candidate: retract exact "seven" and replace by historical seven plus motivic/operadic extension.
