A four-dimensional holomorphic-topological field theory restricts to a
chiral algebra on any holomorphic boundary.  The bar complex of that
chiral algebra governs line defects; the cobar complex reconstructs
bulk local operators; Koszul duality exchanges the two.  The full
development---Swiss-cheese operad, descent calculus, recognition
theorem---is in Volume~II\@.  The bar complex of this volume, equipped
with its coassociative coproduct
(Theorem~\ref{thm:bar-swiss-cheese}), provides the algebraic input.

\subsection{Celestial holography and boundary transfer}
\label{subsec:celestial-boundary-transfer}

The Kaluza--Klein reduction of six-dimensional holomorphic theories to
three-dimensional holomorphic-topological theories produces transferred
$A_\infty$- and $L_\infty$-structures on the tangential Cauchy--Riemann
cohomology $H_b^{0,\bullet}(S^3)$.  These transferred structures are
the algebraic skeleton of celestial holography in the twisted setting.

The \emph{wedge $A_\infty$-algebra} construction quotients by
composite operators and shows that the single-particle celestial
algebra depends only on the linearized dg algebra; all genuinely
nonlinear content is isolated in the composite sector, measured by a
tower of obstruction classes $\operatorname{Ob}_r$ in the bar-filtered
coderivation complex.

For holomorphic BF, the transferred $A_\infty$-algebra admits closed
highest-weight ladder formulas in every arity, governed by simplex
integrals and a simplex summation law.  For Poisson BF, the
odd highest-weight ladder of the transferred $L_\infty$-deformation of
$T^*[1]\operatorname{Ham}(\mathbb{C}^2)$ is canonically realized by
first-order differential operators
$D_m = -\partial_t \circ M_{t^{2m+2}}$ on $\mathbb{C}[t]$---the
odd positive Witt modes acting on the density line (the
\emph{Airy--Witt normal form}).

For $W$-type Poisson vertex algebras, the first nonlinear
obstruction class $\operatorname{Ob}_1$ depends only on the linearized
Lie conformal structure and the lowest nonlinear homogeneous part of
the generator $\lambda$-brackets.  For the classical $W_3$ PVA this
means the first celestial higher-spin correction is controlled entirely
by the quadratic terms $\frac{32}{5c}T^2$ in the
$\{W_\lambda W\}$ bracket---a precise connection to the $W_3$
composite-field structure computed in
\S\ref{sec:w3-composite-complete}.

The wedge construction, obstruction tower, exact ladder theorems,
and Airy--Witt realization are in Volume~II\@.
