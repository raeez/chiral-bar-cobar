\chapter{Physical origins}
\label{chap:physical-origins}
\label{rem:programme-origins}

The algebraic engine of Part~I was not discovered in a vacuum.
Conformal field theory, factorization algebras, holography,
deformation quantization, and twist constructions in quantum field
theory are all contributing tributaries.  Physics demands locality
(the OPE), defect functoriality (line and surface operators),
and anomaly consistency (the central charge and its Koszul dual) ---
and these demands are precisely the axioms that the bar-cobar
machine formalizes.  The full physical narrative, including string
amplitudes, gauge-theory partition functions, and the conformal
bootstrap, has been developed in Volume~II, where the
holomorphic--topological field theories that produce chiral algebras
as factorization algebras on $\mathbb{C} \times \mathbb{R}$ are
treated in their natural habitat.

This chapter has been moved to Volume~II, where the physical
origins of chiral algebras---string amplitudes, gauge theory
partition functions, and conformal bootstrap---are developed
alongside the holomorphic--topological field theories that
produce them as factorization algebras on
$\mathbb{C} \times \mathbb{R}$.
