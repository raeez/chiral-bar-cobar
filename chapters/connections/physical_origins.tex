\chapter{Physical origins}
\label{chap:physical-origins}
\label{rem:programme-origins}

This chapter has been absorbed into Volume~II\@.  The physical
origins of the bar-cobar machine---locality (the OPE), defect
functoriality (line and surface operators), and anomaly consistency
(the central charge and its Koszul dual)---are developed there
alongside the holomorphic--topological field theories that produce
chiral algebras as factorization algebras on
$\mathbb{C} \times \mathbb{R}$.

The algebraic content that was formerly previewed here now appears
in its proved form: the BV--bar identification
(Theorem~\ref{thm:bv-bar-bridge}), the Feynman--bar dictionary
(Theorem~\ref{thm:feynman-bar-bridge}), and the BRST--bar
quasi-isomorphism (Theorem~\ref{thm:brst-bar-genus0}).
