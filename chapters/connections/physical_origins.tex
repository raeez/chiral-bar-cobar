\chapter{Physical origins}
\label{chap:physical-origins}

This chapter has been moved to Volume~II, where the physical
origins of chiral algebras---string amplitudes, gauge theory
partition functions, and conformal bootstrap---are developed
alongside the holomorphic--topological field theories that
produce them as factorization algebras on
$\mathbb{C} \times \mathbb{R}$.
