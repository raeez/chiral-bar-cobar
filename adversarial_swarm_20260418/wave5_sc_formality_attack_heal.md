## 2. Surviving core (Drinfeld-style)

What is truly proved about SC-formality as a class-$G$ characterisation: on the
Gaussian locus (Heisenberg, lattice VOA, free fermion), the ordered-bar data is
generated by the binary two-point kernel, so every connected genus-$0$ tree with
$r \geq 3$ external legs factors through pairings and is itself zero;
consequently $m_k^{\mathrm{SC}} = 0$ for all $k \geq 3$. This is unconditional.
In the reverse direction, SC-formality forces the shadow tower to truncate at
degree~$2$ PROVIDED one has the (uninscribed) two-colour transfer lemma that
promotes a zero open-colour output to a zero ordered tree; this lemma is the
ordered-to-mixed sibling of the averaging surjection, and its inscription is
the genuine mathematical frontier here. The class-$M$ tower is infinite
(infinitely many nonzero higher shadows) by Riccati algebraicity, not
degree-by-degree nonzero.
